EMERSON

Emerson Electric (Asia) Ltc. Scholarship (SY 2018-2019)

Qualifications and Requirements

For Third Year students

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology (BSPsych)
  • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME)
  • Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineer (BSECE)
  • Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE)
  • Must belong to a family whose per capita income is not more that P150,000.00
  • With a minimun general weighted average of 2.50 for Engineering Students and 2.25for four year course stated above
  • Physically and Mentally fit to study
  • Filipino and of good moral character
  • Resident of Metro Manila or a nearby province only
  • Can communicate in the English language
  • No Withdrawn, Dropped or Failed in any Academic and Non-Academic Subjcts

Application Requirements


  • Duly accomplished ACFPI EAP Form-1 and Personal Data Sheet (PDS) with 2 pcs ID pictures
  • Print Screen of Grades (First year to current year)
  • Current Registration Card (to be submitted upon selection)
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • Parents Income Tax Return for the pass year; if none, a Certificate of Indigency from the Barangay

  • Deadline for Submission of Requirements on June 25,2018
